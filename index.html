Asmavati Riverfront

Asmavati Riverfront is a waterfront being developed along the banks of Asmavati river in Porbandar, India. The major objectives of the project are environment improvement, social infrastructure and sustainable development.

Objectives

Objectives

The project aims to provide Porbandar with a meaningful waterfront environment along the banks of the Asmavati River and to redefine an identity of Porbandar around the river. The project looks to reconnect the city with the river and positively transform the neglected aspects of the riverfront.

Environmental Improvement

Reduction in erosion and flood to safeguard the city; sewage diversion to clean the river; water retention and recharge.

Social Infrastructure

Rehabilitation and resettlement of riverbed dwellers and activities; creation of parks and public spaces; provision of socio-cultural amenities for the city.

Sustainable Development

Generation of resources, revitalisation of neighbourhoods.

Entertainment & Facilities

Entertainment & Facilities

  • Parks & Plazas

    The parks shall enhance livability in the neighbourhoods and provide the city with much needed green spaces and respite from the densely built environment. The plazas, on the other hand, will offer public places for social gatherings, and informal activities to take place.

  • Beautiful Gardens

    This garden has been envisioned as a permanent flower garden spread in Approx 100 Sq mt where more than 330 native and exotic flower species. The garden serves as a city-level unique park where visitors can enjoy beautiful flowers of both seasonal and non-seasonal varieties.

  • Sports Facilities

    We invite you to engage in the wide world of water sports - from cricket to golf. Start planning a weekend getaway with a few rounds on our great Adventure sports.

  • Amenities

    The Riverfront Market has been created to house the traditional flea market. The market provides designated vending areas with 50+ vendor platforms, seating areas, paved walkways, food courts, vehicular access, parking and public washrooms. The public areas shall be shaded with over 100 diverse Plants that have been planted.

  • Exhibition Centre

    Exhibition Centre is a trade-fair facility proposed on the eastern bank of the river. It has been envisioned as a multifaceted convention venue that will be equipped with the latest technological equipments and state-of-the-art facilities. The centre shall address the requirements of the exhibitors and visitors alike.

  • Event Centre

    The Events Ground on the riverfront has been planned to offer a wide range of venues for local as well as international events. The centre provides the infrastructural facilities for holding events along the river in an organized manner. It includes a stage with back-stage facilities, VIP Lounge, designated parking, zones for event halls, lawn area and lighting provision.

Attractions

Attractions

Stroll along the Asmavati River on the scenic 2KM Riverfront, and you'll pass something for every mood and every taste, from open green spaces to movie screens, from world-class theatre to minor-league Cricket tournament ground... and dozens of places to eat.

The Garden park on the Riverfront hosts meetings, trade shows, conventions and celebrations right in the heart of the Riverfront. Days on the Riverfront find joggers running along the path from the Triangular Park of Asmavati Riverfront to Chhaya Nagarpalika road, families exploring the Unique Medical Park and businesspeople struggling to decide what's for lunch among the many options inside the Riverfront Market.

At night, the Riverfront pops with activity, from the crack of the bat at triangular park games to standing ovations at the Amphy Theatre Company. And when it's time to eat, everything is within an easy walk â€“local food, kid-friendly quick bites, food trucks, farm-to-table restaurants, hip nightspots, and some of the best outdoor decks in all of Porbandar.
